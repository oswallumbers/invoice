Imported Sawn Size

Precision-cut timber, ready for your application.

What is Sawn Size Timber?

Sawn Size timber, also known as lumber, is raw timber from Imported Round Logs that has been cut or "sawn" into specific dimensional planks, beams, or boards. This is the first step in processing logs into usable material for construction, furniture, and packaging.

At Oswal Lumbers, we operate our own sawmills to produce precision-cut sawn timber. We stock a wide variety of species, including **Pine, Teak, and various Hardwoods,** in standard sizes. We can also provide custom-cut sizes to meet your exact project specifications.

Our Sawn Timber Range

We provide a wide range of sawn timber to cover everything from packaging to premium furniture.

Pine Sawn Size

A versatile and economical softwood, ideal for furniture framing, construction, and the packaging industry (crates, pallets).

Key Features:

  • Lightweight & Easy to Work With
  • Cost-Effective Solution
  • Ideal for Packaging & Framing

Teak Sawn Size

A premium hardwood renowned for its exceptional durability, water resistance, and rich golden-brown color. Perfect for high-end furniture, doors, windows, and outdoor applications.

Key Features:

  • Superior Durability & Longevity
  • Natural Oil & Water Resistance
  • Premium Aesthetic Appeal

Hardwood Sawn Size

Includes species like Eucalyptus, SYP (Southern Yellow Pine), and other durable woods. These are used when strength and structural integrity are paramount.

Key Features:

  • High Structural Strength
  • Excellent Load-Bearing Capacity
  • Ideal for Construction & Beams
