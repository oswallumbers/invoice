Imported Round Logs

Premium Pine and Eucalyptus logs sourced directly from global partners.

What are Imported Round Logs?

Imported Round Logs are the primary raw material for the entire timber industry. These are large, unprocessed logs harvested from sustainably managed forests around the world and imported directly to our facility in Kandla.

By importing logs directly, we maintain complete control over the quality and grading process. These logs are then used for producing our own high-quality Core Veneer and Sawn Size timber, or supplied to other industries.

We specialize in the import of two main categories: **Pine Wood Round Logs** and **Eucalyptus Round Logs**.

Our Round Log Varieties

We import distinct log types to serve different industrial applications, from construction to high-quality veneer.

Pine Wood Round Logs

Pine logs are a versatile softwood known for their uniform grain and lighter weight. We import various species of pine from plantations in New Zealand, the USA, and South America. They are ideal for producing sawn timber for furniture, construction, and packaging.

Key Features:

  • Sustainably Sourced from Plantations
  • Lightweight and Easy to Saw
  • Uniform, Straight Grain
  • Ideal for Sawn Timber & Packaging

Eucalyptus Round Logs

Eucalyptus is a fast-growing hardwood prized for its high density, strength, and durability. We source these logs from South Africa, Brazil, and Uruguay. They are the primary raw material for producing high-strength Euca Core Veneer.

Key Features:

  • High Density & Superior Strength
  • Excellent for Peeling (Veneer)
  • Good Durability & Borer Resistance
  • Perfect for Structural Core Veneer
