Our Legacy in Timber Excellence

Since 1988, Oswal Lumbers Pvt Ltd has been a trusted manufacturer, importer, and exporter in the timber industry. We've grown from a small sawmill into one of the most reliable wood product suppliers in the region.

We specialize in sourcing globally from countries like South Africa, Ghana, New Zealand, Australia, USA, and Malaysia. We process and supply premium products to clients across India and export to markets including the UAE, Saudi Arabia, Oman, and Qatar.

Our commitment to sustainable practices and stringent quality control has earned us a reputation for reliability and excellence, making us a valuable and trusted partner in the industry.
