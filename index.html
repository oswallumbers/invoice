Our Legacy in Timber Excellence

Oswal Lumbers Pvt Ltd has been a trusted name in the timber industry since 1985. What started as a small sawmill has grown into one of the leading wood product manufacturers in the region.

We specialize in sourcing, processing, and supplying premium quality core veneer and imported logs to clients across various industries including plywood manufacturing, construction, and furniture making.

Our commitment to sustainable forestry practices and stringent quality control has earned us a reputation for reliability and excellence in the market.
