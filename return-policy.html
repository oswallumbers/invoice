Return & Refund Policy

Oswal Lumbers Pvt Ltd | Effective Date: 01 Jan 2025

This Return & Refund Policy applies to all products supplied by Oswal Lumbers Pvt Ltd (“Company”, “we”, “our”, “us”) through quotations, invoices, or direct sales.

1. Scope

We deal primarily in timber, wood logs, veneers, and allied industrial products. Due to the nature of goods, returns are limited and governed strictly by this policy.

2. Order Confirmation & Inspection

  • All orders are confirmed through written quotation or invoice.
  • Products undergo quality inspection before dispatch.
  • Customers must inspect goods immediately upon delivery.

3. Returns / Replacements

Eligibility:

  • Goods delivered are damaged, defective, or not as per agreed specifications.
  • Return request raised within 7 days of delivery.
⚠ Requests raised after 7 days from delivery will not be accepted.

Process:

  • Notify us via email or phone with invoice details.
  • Share photographs/videos clearly showing the issue.
  • Goods must be returned with original packaging and documents.

4. Inspection & Approval

All returned goods will be inspected at our facility. Replacement or refund will be processed only after verification.

5. Refunds

  • Refunds are issued only if replacement is not possible.
  • Refunds may be adjusted against future orders or processed via bank transfer.
  • Processing time: up to 14 business days after inspection.

6. Shipping Charges

Shipping costs for returns are borne by the customer unless the return is due to incorrect or damaged goods supplied by us.

7. Non-Returnable Items

  • Custom-cut or made-to-order products.
  • Goods accepted and acknowledged by the customer.
  • Products altered, used, or damaged after delivery.

8. Order Cancellation

  • Cancellations are allowed only before dispatch.
  • Processing or handling charges may apply.
  • Orders once dispatched cannot be cancelled.
