Face Veneer

The perfect aesthetic finish for your premium products.

What is Face Veneer?

Face Veneer is a very thin sheet of premium, decorative wood (typically 0.25mm to 0.6mm thick) that is applied to the visible outer surfaces of engineered wood panels like plywood, MDF, or particleboard.

While Core Veneer provides structural strength from the inside, Face Veneer provides aesthetic beauty on the outside. It gives the final product the beautiful, natural appearance of solid wood at a fraction of the cost.

We supply two main types: **Natural Wood Veneer**, which showcases unique, real wood grains, and **Reconstituted (Recon) Veneer**, which is engineered for a consistent, uniform pattern.

Our Face Veneer Varieties

We provide a wide selection of species and styles to achieve any desired look for your furniture or panels.

Natural Wood Veneer

Sliced directly from a log, Natural Veneer retains the unique, authentic grain pattern and warm color of the original wood. Each sheet is one-of-a-kind. We supply popular species like Gurjan, Teak, Oak, and Walnut.

Key Features:

  • Authentic, Unique Wood Grain
  • Rich Natural Colors & Tones
  • Timeless, High-End Appearance
  • Ideal for Premium Furniture & Paneling

Reconstituted (Recon) Veneer

Recon Veneer is an engineered product made from real, fast-growing wood. The wood is dyed, laminated, and re-sliced to create a consistent, defect-free grain pattern. It's an eco-friendly choice that guarantees uniformity across large projects.

Key Features:

  • Consistent, Uniform Grain Pattern
  • Defect-Free (No knots or splits)
  • Eco-Friendly & Sustainable
  • Perfect for Large-Scale Projects
